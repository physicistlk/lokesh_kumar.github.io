Major Milestone

Neuromorphic Hardware Breakthrough

Demonstrated bio-realistic emulation of complex synaptic plasticity and logic (Ebbinghaus forgetting curve, STDP, paired-pulse facilitation, and OR logic) in a low-energy (~24 nJ) fully screen-printed organic electrochemical transistor (OECT).

Carbon Journal Publication

Fabricated low-voltage photo-thermally reduced graphene oxide (rGO) transistors using an ion gel dielectric. The photo-thermal method avoids toxic chemical reagents and yields superior carrier mobility and stability compared to traditional reduction methods.

Chemical Science Breakthrough

Developed an ultrasensitive OECT biosensor for the detection of aromatic water pollutants at parts-per-billion (ppb) levels through controlled protein immobilization. Targeted real-time environmental monitoring.
